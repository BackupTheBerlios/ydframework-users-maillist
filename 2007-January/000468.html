Hi guys,

It would really be easy for users and beginners to get resources from a
forum because they can view those public postings without logging in or
registering. However, I prefer mailing list for developers, but forum for
users and beginners who wants to learn more and discover some experiences
with other users of ydframework . Having a mailing list, we have to log-in
for our email account just to see emails from yellowduck framework, while in
forum we can view while not logging in and its categorized.

Maybe we can have some also a test for a year if having a forum is really
effective. I have also found on this site (
http://www.phpkitchen.com/index.php?/archives/669-PHP-Frameworks.html).
YDFramework is found on the "Interesting But Couldn't Find Reviews" section.
Hope having a forum can help to provide more reviews on it.

@Pieter: Release of YDFramework 2.1 is so great!!! Great great job!

regards,

paul toytoy
