Hi dab,

I'm not sure why that happens, or if it is as intended, but what you're 
trying to do can be done with:

$user = YDDatabaseObject::getInstance( 'users' );
$user->id = 1;
$user->username = 'dab';
$user->update();

Provided that 'id' is the key field.

Good luck!
Peter
