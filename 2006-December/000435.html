Hello all,

I've got a mod_rewrite rule that reads as follows:
RewriteRule ^[Cc]atalogue/[Rr]eadywear/([A-Za-z_-]+)/([A-Za-z_-]+)/? 
$ /catalogue/index.php?cat=$1&sub_cat=$2 [L]

so I have links that looks like /Catalogue/Readywear/Neckties/Silk/  
or /Catalogue/Readywear/Shirts/Button_Down/


the problem I have is that If I introduce a recordset into the  
equation the links generated by YDRecordset with {$highlights- 
 >getNextUrl()} look something like: /Catalogue/Readywear/Neckties/ 
Silk/?page=2&size=4 which seems to mangle the whole rewrite rule and  
renders the pagination inactive.

Do you have any suggestions as to how achieve clean urls whilst  
retaining the recordset functionality, something like:

/Catalogue/Readywear/Neckties/Silk/2/4/


any other possible solutions are also welcome.

many thanks.
