Sure.. with Yellow Duck Framework you can do it easily ;)


Instead of:

<span class="test"> {$form.name.label} </span>




You just need:

{if ($form.errors.name)} <span 
style="color:#FF0000">{$form.name.label_html}</span>
{else} {$form.name.label_html} {/if}
