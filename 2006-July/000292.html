hi moses,
i could be wrong (and i guess somebody will correct me
if that's the case) but looking at the
YDFormRenderer_html.php it doesn't seem to support
that.
You could hack it a bit though and create your own
form renderer by doing:

$form->registerRenderer('whatever','MyRenderer',
'renderers/MyRenderer.php');	
$this->template->assign( 'form',
$form->render('whatever') );

hope that helps.

/dab
